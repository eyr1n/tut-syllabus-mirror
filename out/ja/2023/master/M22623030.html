授業の目標
日本語
固体の物理現象、特に光の関与に関する物理現象を理解し、それらの現象のデバイスへの応用のされ方について学ぶことにより、光量子デバイスを開発、発展させていく能力を身につける

Lectures by Prof. Ishikawa
1st week: Wave Equation, Plane Wave
2nd week: Dielectric Function (Lorentz Model), Refractive Index
3rd week: Fresnel Reflection 1 (Boundary Condition, Normal Incidence, Anti-reflection Coating)
4th week: Fresnel Reflection 2 (Incidence Angle Dependence, Total Internal Reflection))
5th week: Practice
6th week: Dielectric Multilayer, Transfer Matrix Method
7th week: Dielectric Optical Waveguides
8th week: Interim Exam

Lectures by Prof. Sekiguchi
9th week: Basis of Light emitting devices
10th week: Emission mechanism of Semiconductors (1)
11th week: Emission mechanism of Semiconductors (2)
12th week: Fabrication technology of Laser Diode
13th week: Various laser structure
14th week: Practice
15th week: Recent topics
16th week: Final Exam (Oral examination might be conducted)
