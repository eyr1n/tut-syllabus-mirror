授業の目標
日本語
英　語
　Understand mechanical properties of structural materials, such as lattice defects, strength and fracture, from a microstructural perspective, and also chemical composition and heat treatment procedure of steels and non-ferrous materials including the mechanisms to control mechanical properties.
 
授業の内容
日本語
英　語
【In-person】 [ 01st ] Chap. 1 ~ 4: Introduction of materials science and engineering, Crystal structures in metals and alloys
【In-person】 [ 02nd ] Chap. 4: Crystal structures in metals and alloys
【In-person】 [ 03rd ] Chap. 6: Imperfections in metals and alloys
【In-person】 [ 04th ] Chap. 7: Diffusion in metals and alloys
【In-person】 [ 05th ] Chap. 8, 9: Mechanical properties, Strengthening mechanisms in metals and alloys
【In-person】 [ 06th ] Chap. 9, 10: Strengthening mechanisms, Failure in metals and alloys
【In-person】 [ 07th ] Chap. 11, 12: Phase Diagrams, Phase transformations in metals and alloys
【In-person】 [ 08th ] Review (45min) & Periodic exam. (45min)
