授業の目標
日本語
英　語
The course is intended for students to foster their interests in research problems on computer science and engineering and to acquire ability for independent studies.
It is also aimed for students to acquire, through thesis research, cooperativeness, a sense of responsibility, abilities for problem solving, research planning, decision making, outcome presentation and subject investigation, and to enhance their creativity and persistency, among others.
