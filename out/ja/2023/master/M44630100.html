授業の内容
日本語
英　語
(face to face ) 1. Basic concept of asymmetric synthesis.
(face to face ) 2. Basic concept of Lewis acid catalysis.
(on-demand) 3. Advanced Lewis acid catalysis.
(face to face ) 4. Basic concept of organocatalyst.
(on-demand) 5. Advanced organocatalysis in organic synthesis.
(face to face ) 6. Asymmetric synthesis of halogenated compounds.
(on-demand) 7. Advanced asymmetric synthesis.
(face-to-face) 8. Review and examination
