授業の目標
日本語
英　語
Students will acquire the following skills by taking this course.
1) Learn basic knowledge of machines for precision and ultra‒precision motions, and gain an understanding of precision deterioration factors.
2) Gain an understanding of basic principles of sensors and properties of control methods for precision and ultra‒precision motions, and be able to choose appropriately.
3) Learn typical mathematical programming approaches that optimize objective functions under constraints.

達成目標
日本語
英　語
1) To understand the basics of precision mechatronic systems and have a brief discussion.
2) To understand and explain the basic features of mechanisms and actuators in precision mechatronic systems and the differences in selection depending on the working range.
3) To understand and use the basic features of sensors for precision mechatronic systems.
4) To understand control issues in precision mechatronic systems.
5) Expected to understand fundamentals of mathematical programming
6) Expected to understand fundamentals of linear programming
7) Expected to understand fundamentals of nonlinear programming
