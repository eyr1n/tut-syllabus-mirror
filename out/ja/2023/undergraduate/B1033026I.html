達成目標
日本語
1. 英語を聴く力及び話す力を養う。特に，文章が伝える情報をはやく正確につかむ力及び意思を的確に表現する力を養う。
2. 自分の伝えたいことを表現するために単語力を増強する。
英　語
1. To develop English listening and speaking skill. Above all, this course focuses on obtaining the ability to understand spoken information quickly and accurately, and to speak proper English expressing ones’ own thought.
2. To increase vocabulary as the basis of the ability to express oneself.

Students will be evaluated according to assessment 20％，E-Learning 20%， assignments (20%), class participation (20%), and the score of the examination(20%).

Evaluation criteria

Students who meet required attendance will be evaluated as follows:

S: Total points 90 or higher (out of 100 points).
A: Total points 80 or higher (out of 100 points).
B: Total points 70 or higher (out of 100 points).
C: Total points 60 or higher (out of 100 points).

Grade distribution:
S: 90% and more
A: 80-89%
B: 70-79%
C: 60-69%
