授業の目標
日本語
基本的なフランス語の文法を理解し、簡単なフランス語を聞き、話し、読み、書く力を習得する。
フランス語の学習を通じて、フランスの文化・社会に対する理解を深める。
英　語
To understand basic French grammar and acquire simple French listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills.
To enhance understanding of the French culture and society through French language study.
 
授業の内容
日本語
本授業は「対面あるいはオンデマンド」形式で行う予定です。

会話文の発音・書き取り、文法のまとめ、演習問題を行い、コミュニケーションに役立つ構文や表現を学ぶ。
映像資料により、フランスの生活・文化に親しむ。

教科書の第0課から第7課までを学習する。
（対面あるいはオンデマンド）第１回　第0課　フランスとフランス語に親しむ (アルファベ)

（対面あるいはオンデマンド）第３回　第1課　挨拶する (発音と綴り字))

英　語
The classes are to be held in the style of face to face or on-demand (You can take the class whenever you want.).

The class will consist of pronouncing and dictating conversational sentences, grammatical summary, and solving practice problems, through which the students will learn various sentence structures and expressions that will be useful in actual French communication.
Video materials will be used to familiarize the students with the French lifestyle and culture.
The class will cover Lessons 0 through 7 of the textbook.

(face to face or on-demand)1st lesson, Lesson 0 - Become Familiar with France and French (alphabet)
(face to face or on-demand)2nd lesson, Lesson 1- To Greet (pronunciation and spelling)
(face to face or on-demand)3rd lesson, Lesson 1 – To Greet (pronunciation and spelling)
(face to face or on-demand)4th lesson, Lesson 2 – To Say name, nationality and profession (personal pronouns subjects, verb “être”- to be-)
(face to face or on-demand)5th lesson, Lesson 2 – To Say name, nationality and profession (adjective agreement with number and gender)
(face to face or on-demand)6th lesson, Lesson 3 – To Tell the age, To Talk about the family (number and gender of nouns, indefinite article)
(face to face or on-demand)7th lesson, Lesson 3 – To Tell the age, To Talk about the family (verb “avoir”-to have-, negative phrases)
(face to face or on-demand)8th lesson, Lesson 4 – To Say what you like (definite article, 1st group verbs)
(face to face or on-demand)9th lesson, Lesson 4 – To Say what you like (placement of adjectives)
(face to face or on-demand)10th lesson, Lesson 5 – To Say what you have (demonstrative adjectives, possessive adjectives )
(face to face or on-demand)11th lesson, Lesson 5 – To Say what you have (tonic personal pronouns)
(face to face or on-demand)12th lesson, Lesson 6 – To Talk about friends (placement of adjectives)
(face to face or on-demand)13th lesson, Lesson 6 – To Talk about friends (adjective agreement with number and gender)
(face to face or on-demand)14th lesson, Lesson 7 – To Ask (interrogative adverb, interrogative adjectives)
(face to face or on-demand)15th lesson, Summary
(face to face or on-demand)16th lesson, Final examination (or Assignment)

達成目標
日本語
(1)平易なフランス語を読み、その内容を理解することができる。
(2)正確な文法に基づいた初歩的なフランス語を書くことができる。
(3)フランス語による平易な会話を聞き取ることができる。
(4)フランス語で簡単な日常会話ができる。
英　語
(1) Read simple French sentences and understand their meaning.
(2) Write rudimentary French sentences based on correct grammar usage.
(3) Listen to simple French conversations and understand their meaning.
(4) Have ordinary and simple conversation using everyday French expressions.
