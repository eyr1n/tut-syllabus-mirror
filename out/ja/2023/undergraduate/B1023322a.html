授業の目標
日本語
精神疾患（精神障害，精神病，こころの病とほぼ同義）はありふれた病気である．近年の日本の疫学調査によれば，精神疾患の年間有病率（当該1年間に疾患を有した時期のある者の割合）は約10％と推定される．したがって，精神疾患は誰にとっても決して他人事ではない．しかし，残念なことに，世間に流布している精神疾患の膨大な情報（たとえばインターネットを見よ）はしばしば混乱している．そこで，講師は一介の精神科臨床医として，精神疾患の実像を要約・紹介する．
英　語
Mental illness ( roughly synonymous with mental disorder, mental disease, psychotic disorder and "Kokoro-no-Yamai" ) is a common disease. Recent epidemiological researches suggest that one-year prevalence ( the proportion of a population that has the condition of specific disease at some time during a given year ) of the mental illness is approximately 10% in Japan. Therefore, mental illness is by no means other people's affairs for everyone. Unfortunately, however, common information about the illness, for example in Internet, are huge and often confused. As a psychiatric clinician, the lecturer will summarize and introduce the real picture of mental disorders.
 
授業の内容
日本語
授業回数は7.5 回．
講義資料は印刷物およびGoogle Classroom (pdf file) により提供する．
定期試験を実施しない．

1:導入　精神疾患の印象，精神科と心療内科の異同，入院中心主義
2:脳病態（精神疾患の脳病態を4型に分類する）
3:診断・疫学　国際標準診断基準(DSM,ICD)，有病率，受療行動
4:神経症（脳病態4型の疾患）パニック障害，社交不安障害，強迫性障害など
5:気分障害（脳病態3型の疾患）うつ病，双極性障害など
6:統合失調症（脳病態3型の疾患） 4症状群，短期・中期・長期経過
7:補足・総括（１）日本近代精神医療史，遺伝要因と環境要因，自殺
7.5:補足・総括（２）サイコバブル，精神病様体験

医師として精神科医療に40年以上携わっている教員が精神医学の基礎的知識について講義する．
英　語
Lecture(7.5)
All lectures materials will be provided in print and by Google classroom (pdf file).
No exams are required during the exam period.

1:introduction impressions of mental illness, differences between psychiatry and psychosomatic medicine, hospitalization centricity.
2:brain pathology ( classified into 4 types ) of mental illness.
3:diagnosis and epidemiology international standard diagnostic criteria(DSM,ICD), prevalence, medical treatment behavior.
4:neurosis ( type4 illness )　panic disorder, social anxiety disorder, obsessive-compulsive disorder, etc.
5:mood disorder ( type3 illness ) major depressive disorder, bipolar affective disorder, etc.
6:schizophrenia ( type3 illness ) major 4 syndromes, short-mid-long term course.
7:supplement and summary(1) history of modern Japanese psychiatry, genetic and environmental factors, suicide.
7.5:supplement and summary(2) psychobabble, psychotic-like experiences.

A doctor who has been engaged in psychiatry for over 40 years will give a lecture on basic knowledge of psychiatry.
