達成目標
日本語
建設法規の社会的役割が理解できること。建築基準法の基本的構成と具体的規制内容の基礎が理解できること。建設法規の学びを通して、技術者の社会に対する貢献と責任を理解できること。
英　語
1. to be able to understand the social role and social responsibility.
2. to be able to understand fundamental structure and regulation contents in detail on building regulation law.
3. to be able to understand the contributions and responsibilities of engineers to society by studying building regulation law.
