授業の目標
日本語
Reading, Writing, Speaking, Listeningのバランスに配慮しつつ、これまでに身につけた英語のうち特にListening及びSpeakingをさらに強化する。
英　語
With concern for a balance of reading, writing, speaking and listening, we especially aim to improve Listening and Speaking skill of each student.
 
授業の内容
日本語
Week 1: Introduction, “Please call me…”(face to face)
Week 2: Conversation, “Do you like sushi?”(face to face)
Week 3: Small talk, “Lovely weather today, isn’t it?”(face to face)
Week 4: Forming a Bond, “I like surfing too.”(face to face)
Week 5: Taking Notes and Paraphrasing, “To summarize the meeting…”(face to face)
Week 6: Describing Things, “What is that?”(face to face)
Week 7: Describing your Job, “What do you do?”(face to face)
Week 8: The Present, “I study English. I am studying verb tenses.”(face to face)
Week 9: Past Events I, “Last weekend I went to…”(face to face)
Week 10: Active Listening, “Could you please speak a little slower?”(face to face)
Week 11: Phone Calls, “How can I be of service?”(face to face)
Week 12: Requests, “My pen is broken. Could I borrow your pen please?”(face to face)
Week 13: Agreeing and Disagreeing I, “I agree, but…”(face to face)
Week 14: Meeting "Do you have an opinion about..." (face to face)
Week 15: Job Interviews, “What do you see yourself doing in 5 years?”(face to face)
Week 16: Final Assessment(face to face)


If there are any changes to the course schedule, the information will be on Google Classroom or KYOMU JOHO SYSTEM.
There may be changes to course content and academic assessment methods following changes to activities to prevent the spread of COVID-19.

成績の評価法(定期試験、課題レポート等の配分)および評価基準
日本語
評価法：assessment(in class) 35％，assessment(final) 25%，授業への貢献度20％の割合で評価する, Gyutto-e online course 20%。

原則的に全ての講義に出席したものにつき、全ての達成目標の達成度を下記のように評価する。

　S：レポート・期末テストの合計点が90点（100点満点）以上
　A：レポート・期末テストの合計点が80点（100点満点）以上
　B：レポート・期末テストの合計点が70点（100点満点）以上
　C：レポート・期末テストの合計点が60点（100点満点）以上
英　語
Students will be evaluated according to assessments(in class) (35%), assessment(final) (25%), class participation (20%), Gyutto-e online course (20%).


Evaluation criteria

Students who meet required attendance will be evaluated as follows:


S: Total points obtained from exams and/or reports, etc., 90 or higher (out of 100 points).
A: Total points obtained from exams and/or reports, etc., 80 or higher (out of 100 points).
B: Total points obtained from exams and/or reports, etc., 70 or higher (out of 100 points).
C: Total points obtained from exams and/or reports, etc., 60 or higher (out of 100 points).

Grade distribution:
S: 90% and more
A: 80-89%
B: 70-79%
C: 60-69%

The term exam covers all the contents listed in “Goals to be achieved.” Attendance policy is announced at the orientation in April.
