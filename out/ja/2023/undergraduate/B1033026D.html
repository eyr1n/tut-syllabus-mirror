授業の内容
日本語
1. Orientation (contact information, schedule, and evaluation)
2. Unit 1: What’s your name? (Introduce yourself to a partner)
3. Unit 2: I love fashion! (Describe the clothes you are wearing)
4. Unit 3: How do you stay healthy? (Give advice about staying healthy)
5. Unit 4: How do I get there? (Ask for and give directions to a place)
6. Unit 5: What’s that? (Describe different objects)
7. Unit 6: What’s your dream? (Talk about your goals)
8. Review
9. Unit 7: It was great! (Talk about your past experiences)
10. Unit 8: How much do you know? (Describe animals and nature)
11. Unit 9: She can really sing! (Talk about things you can and can’t do)
12. Unit 10: What do you like to do? (Ask about likes and dislikes)
13. Unit 11: Of course you can (Talk about rules and laws in other countries)
14. Unit 12: What happened next? (Make up a story and tell it to your partner)
15. Review
16. Examination
