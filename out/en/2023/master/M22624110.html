Objectives of class
Japanese
工学部での高周波回路工学に引き続き，マイクロ波線形回路の動作原理と設計技術を習得する．
English
To extend the knowledge you got in an undergraduate class on radio frequency circuits, students learn the operating principles and design techniques of linear microwave circuits.
 
Contents of class
Japanese
RFフロントエンドの設計開発業務に携わった経験を持つ教員がRFフロントエンドの設計に関する基礎知識について講義する．

７週目　フィルタ設計

English
Teachers with experience in the RF front-end circuits will speak about basic knowledge related to the basic knowledge of RF front-end circuits.
1st wk. Transmission line
2nd wk. Waveguide and modes
3rd wk. Coupled line and directional coupler
4th wk. Application of transmission line 1
5th wk. Application of transmission line 2
6th wk. Loaded, unloaded and external Q factors of resonator
7th wk. Filter design
8th wk. Term exam
