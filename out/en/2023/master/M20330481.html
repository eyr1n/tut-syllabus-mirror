Objectives of class
Japanese
戦後のアメリカ文学やコミックスの歴史を辿りながら、アメリカの消費文化の変遷について理解を深める。
English
This course aims to deepen understanding of transformation of American consumer culture by exploring the history of American literature and comics since the postwar era.

Japanese

English
Students will be evaluated according to a final report (50%), mid-term report (20%), and other assignments and contributions to the class (30%).

Grade Distribution:
S: 90% or above
A: 80-89%
B: 70-79%
C: 60-69%
