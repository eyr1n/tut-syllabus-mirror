Objectives of class
Japanese

　超臨界流体工学および環境化学工学の観点から、実践的創造的かつ指導的技術者として必要な廃棄物の再資源化およびバイオマス利活用に関する知識を習得し、様々な課題を克服する基礎的能力を養う。
English

　Based on Supercritical Fluid Engineering and Environmental Chemical Engineering, practical philosophy, creativity and leadership of engineer are improved during this course. The topics are mainly waste management and utilization of biomass. Environmental issue is widely discussed to obtain the knowledge and organizing skill of comprehensive process or society.
