Objectives of class
Japanese
This course is designed to introduce you to the scientific study of human nature. You will learn why and how scientists ask question about the sensation and perception and the relation of brain and behavior.You will also learn about the research methods to measure the perception and cognition used in the field of psychology and cognitive science.Finally, you will be able to create your own experiments using the ‘OpenSesame’, worldwide well-known software for creating experiments for psychology, cognitive science, neuroscience and experimental economics.
English
This course is designed to introduce you to the scientific study of human nature. You will learn why and how scientists ask question about the sensation and perception and the relation of brain and behavior.You will also learn about the research methods to measure the perception and cognition used in the field of psychology and cognitive science.Finally, you will be able to create your own experiments using the ‘OpenSesame’, worldwide well-known software for creating experiments for psychology, cognitive science, neuroscience and experimental economics.
