Objectives of class
Japanese
磁性体はコンピュータのハードディスク，自動車などで使われているモータ，電子機器中で用いられている変圧器，その他様々な永久磁石など，我々の身の回りで多数用いられている．本講義は磁性体の基礎から応用まで講義し，磁性体が起こす現象を正しく理解し，応用ができるようにすることを目指す．
English
Magnetic materials are commonly used in our life, such as computers hard disks, motors for automobiles, transformers, permanent magnets and so on. In the magnetism, I will explain from fundamentals to applications of the magnetic material, aim to make you understand the phenomena of magnetic materials correctly.

English
Teacher with experience in light and device will speak about basic knowledge related to magnetism.

3rd week: Crystal field and d-electrons

Goals to be achieved
Japanese

(3)磁性体の各種現象を深く理解し，応用できるようにする．
English
(1) Understand the fundamental physics of magnetism correctly.
(2) Understand magnetic phenomena by using knowledge of quantum mechanics
(3) Understand various phenomena of magnetic material and be able to use magnetic materials for research and work.
