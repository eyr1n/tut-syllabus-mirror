Objectives of class
Japanese
English
As a stepping stone to a Master's research, a preliminary project involving measurement experiments or system development is carried out under the supervision of a supervisor. After a necessary and sufficient survey of the relevant research, students define the research question, consider the appropriate research methods and discuss the impact of the results obtained. A presentation opportunity is given at the end of the semester to exchange the ideas among other students and supervisors. Master's research may be carried out by improving/expanding the project.
 
Contents of class
Japanese
English
The project theme is initially presented as a candidate by the supervisors and is finally decided through discussion with the students.
