Objectives of class
Japanese
本講義では、学部で学習した電磁気学・量子力学などの基礎に基づき、材料エレクトロニクス分野の研究に欠かせない材料特性および作製プロセスの基礎に関わる基礎について学習する。
English
Based on the fundamental lecture including electromagnetism and quantum mechanics in undergraduate, this lecture provides fundamentals of materials properties and their fabrication process for your study on Electronic Materials course.
 
Contents of class
Japanese
１週目　光の吸収と放出
２週目　光吸収の基礎：半導体、金属、分子、有機材料
３週目　光化学プロセスの基礎１：光CVD、光ゾルゲル、光加工
４週目　光化学プロセスの基礎２：光CVD、光ゾルゲル、光加工
５週目　光反応の基礎１：光触媒、光合成
６週目　光反応の基礎２：光触媒、光合成
７週目　ナノ光励起プロセス
８週目　まとめ＋レポート課題説明

本学の新型コロナウィルス感染拡大防止のための活動基準の変更に伴い、授業内容および成績の評価法に変更が生じる場合があります。
English
1st week: Absorption and emission of light
2nd week: Fundamentals of absorption on semiconductor, metal, molecule, organic material
3rd week: Fundamentals of photo-chemical process 1 on photo-CVD, photo-assisted sol-gel, photo-chemical process
4th week: Fundamentals of photo-chemical process 2 on photo-CVD, photo-assisted sol-gel, photo-chemical process
5th week: Fundamentals of photo-process 1 on photo-catalyst and photosynthesis
6th week: Fundamentals of photo-process 2 on photo-catalyst and photosynthesis
7th week: Photo-excitation process in nano-scale
8th week: Summary and the instruction about the report
