Objectives of class
Japanese
企業経営や地域振興にうおいて戦略的なデザイン活用の重要性が増しています。デザインの捉え方、デザインの営みや拡がり、デザイナーの職能等を理解し、企業や地域における有効なデザインの活用への視角を得ることを目指す。
English
The importance of strategic design utilization is increasing in corporate management and regional development. We aim to gain a perspective on the effective use of design in companies and regions by understanding how to perceive design, the activities and spread of design, and the profession of designers.
