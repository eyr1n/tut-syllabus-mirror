Objectives of class
Japanese
English
The course is intended for students to study basic materials in depth, related to his/her research topics through the lab works. It is also aimed for students to acquire various skills, required in general research work, such as those for literature review, oral presentation, and technical discussion and writing.
 
Contents of class
Japanese
English
While specific contents depend on the research topics students are involved in, it is usually the case for students to read relevant textbooks/research papers and report on them, as well as to present and discuss on the research work of their own.
