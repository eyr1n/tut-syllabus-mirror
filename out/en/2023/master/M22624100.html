Objectives of class
Japanese
工学部での高周波回路工学に引き続き、マイクロ波能動素子受動素子およびこれらを用いる非線形回路の動作原理と設計技術を習得する。
English
To extend the knowledge you got in an undergraduate class on radio frequency circuits, students learn the operating principles and design techniques of non-linear microwave circuits.
