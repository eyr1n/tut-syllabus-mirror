Objectives of class
Japanese
機械工学系の各研究分野に関する基礎から最新に?までの知識を習得する．セミナー形式の輪講を行うことにより，単なる講義では身につけることが難しい問題意識，問題解決力，課題探求力，判断力，プレゼンテーション力を身につける．
English
Knowledge from fundamental to advanced levels are acquired in each research field of mechanical engineering. Abilities for problem-solving, problem-questing, and judgement, and presentation skill are polished up at seminar of this class.

Goals to be achieved
Japanese
(1) 各研究分野において研究を遂行するために必要な基礎から最新に至までの知識を習得する．
(2) 文献の内容を正確に理解し，端的に紹介できる．

English
(1) Knowledge from fundamental to advanced levels is acquired in each research field of mechanical engineering.to perform research.
(2) Contents of literature are understood and presented accurately and briefly.
(3) Problem-setting is found by developing content of literature.
