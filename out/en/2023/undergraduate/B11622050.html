Objectives of class
Japanese
金属加工の一つである接合加工のなかの固相接合，ろう接，接着接合，機械的締結，表面処理について，その基本原理や適用例などについて学ぶ．
English
Learn fundamental principles and applications of solid state welding, brazing, adhesive bonding, mechanical fastening, surface treatment as a normal joining technology for the metallic materials.
 
Contents of class
Japanese
