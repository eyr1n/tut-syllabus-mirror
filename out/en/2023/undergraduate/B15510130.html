Objectives of class
Japanese
建築設計とはさまざまな専門知識や技術を総合して形態及び空間を創造することであり、その表現手段として設計図・模型・透視図などがある。また、この表現手段は設計者の意図を相手に的確に伝える役割も果たし、意匠・構造・設備全ての設計行為のみならず施工も含めた建築にかかわる者にとっての必修の能力である。建築設計学習の第一歩として、建築を創造するための手法である設計製図を習得し、同時に建築にかかわる技術者としての基礎能力を養う。
English
Architectural design is the creation of form and space through the synthesis of various specialized knowledge and techniques, and the means of expression include blueprints, models, and perspective drawings. These means of expression also play a role in accurately communicating the designer's intentions to others, and are essential skills for anyone involved in architecture, including not only design, structure, and equipment design, but also construction. As the first step in learning architectural design, students will master design and drafting, a method for creating architecture, and at the same time, develop basic skills as engineers involved in architecture.
