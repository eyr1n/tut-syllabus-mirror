Objectives of class
Japanese
流体エネルギーと機械的エネルギー相互の変換をする流体機械(ターボ機械など）について，その基礎理論や特性を学ぶ．
English
Students acquire knowledge of fundamental principles and characteristics of fluid machinery such as turbomachinery in which mechanical and fluid energy are mutually converted.
 
Contents of class
Japanese
1週：流体機械の分類，ターボ機械の概要
2週：非圧縮性流体・圧縮性流体のエネルギー変換，効率
3週：遠心式ターボ機械の作動原理
4週：軸流式ターボ機械の作動原理
5週：相似則と性能曲線
6週：軸流機械
7週：流体機械の特異現象
8週：復習，定期試験

English
1st week :Classification of fluid machinery and introduction to turbomachinery
2nd week :Energy conversion for incompressible and compressible flows, and efficiencies
3rd week :Working principle of radial turbomachine
4th week :Working principle of axial turbomachine
5th week :Similarity rule and performance curve
6th week :Axial fluid machinery
7th week :Anomalous phenomena in fluid machinery
8th week :Review and examination

Goals to be achieved
Japanese
(1)流体機械の作動原理と種類を理解する．
(2)ターボ機械における損失を理解する．
(3)ターボ機械の効率や動力を求めることができる．
(4)ターボ機械の性能曲線を理解する．
(5)ターボ機械の相似則を理解する．
English
(1)To understand types and working principles of turbomachines
(2)To understand energy losses in turbomachines
(3)To be able to calculate efficiency, shaft power or hydraulic power, etc. of turbomachines
(4)To understand performance curve of turbomachines
(5)To understand similarity of turbomachines
