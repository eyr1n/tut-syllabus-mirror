Objectives of class
Japanese
文章を要約する作業を通じて、表現に対する意識を高める。
敬語の正しい使い方を学ぶ。
English
To inculcate the Japanese language ability required that of a college student, and the ability to express him/herself and fundamental communication skills of a researcher and a working adult.
Passage comprehension, writing of thesis and reports, content summarizing, presentation and debate take place in order to stretch the abilities to read, write, listen and speak.
Teaching materials include the use of literature, commentary works and newspaper and journal articles. Students also get to learn how to write resumes, entry sheets, business documents and also the appropriate use of polite expressions.
