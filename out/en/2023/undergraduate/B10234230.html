Objectives of class
Japanese
心理学や社会学など多くの領域で学際的な研究が進む消費者行動論について、モノづくりに生かすための基礎概念、諸理論を理解する。消費者ニーズや購買意思決定など基本的概念に加えて、幸福や贅沢など、解釈学的アプローチなども解説し、売れるモノづくりの実践力を身に付ける。
English
Understand the basic concepts and theories for making use of consumer behavior theory, which is being studied interdisciplinarily in many fields such as psychology and sociology. In addition to basic concepts such as consumer needs and purchasing decision making, we will also explain hermeneutic approaches such as happiness and luxury, and acquire the practical skills of manufacturing that will sell.
