Objectives of class
Japanese
電磁気学は，電気・電子情報工学分野の中でも最も重要な基礎科目です。電界、磁界および電磁界を記述する方程式の基
礎から応用までを学びます。講義は現象論の立場から、実験事実に基づいて、微分・積分形の式を用いて説明し、電気・電
子情報工学の基礎的問題を考える力を養います。高専本科卒業生の電磁気レベルを想定し、３年生以降で学習する電磁気
学の理解を深めるための基礎を確立します。
English
Electromagnetism is the most important basic subject in the field of electrical, electronic and information engineering. This
course covers equations from the basics to an advanced level to describe an electric field, magnetic field and
electromagnetic field. The lectures will help students to develop their ability to think about the basic problems of electric
and electronic information engineering by explaining them phenomenologically with the use of differential-integral formulae
based on facts. This course is designed to establish the basics in order to deepen understanding about electromagnetism,
which will be learned in the third year or later, assuming the electromagnetism level for technical college graduates.
 
Contents of class
Japanese

第２週　ガウスの定理と微分形ガウスの法則
第３週　電位とストークスの定理
第５週　誘電体と誘電体を含むコンデンサ
第６週　定常電流と定常電流保存則
第７週　中間試験
第８週　アンペールの力、静磁界の基本法則
第９週　磁性体と磁界、静磁界の基本法則
第10週　ローレンツ力
第11週　電荷保存則と変位電流
第12週　電磁誘導
第13週　運動電磁誘導
第14週　マクスウェルの方程式
第15週　総合まとめ
第16週　期末試験

English
(1) Guidance, Fundamentals of vector analysis, Electric field and Guss's law
(2) Gauss's theorem and differential Guss's law
(3) Electrostatic potential and Stoke's theorem
(4) Electrostatic energy and the fundamental laws of electrostatic field
(5) Dielectric materials and the condensers with dielectric materials
(6) Steady current and the conservation law of steady current
(7) Midterm exam.
(8) Amper's law and the fundamental laws of the magnetostatic field
(9) Magnetic materials and magnetic field, and the fundamenatal laws of the magnetostatic field
(10) Lorentz force
(11) The conservation law of charge and displacement current
(12) Electromagnetic induction
(13) Motion electromagnetic induction
(14) Maxwell's equations
(15) Summary
(16) Final exam.
