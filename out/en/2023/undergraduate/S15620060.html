From the point of view of the common people, we deal with themes related to the history of architectural culture in Japan. Watch video materials related to the theme and have a discussion to deepen your understanding.

3. Preserving and Regenerating Historic Townscapes
4. Kyoto-Style Traditional Townhouse and Tsuboniwa
5. Kagai and Traditional Japanese Bar
6. Traditional Townhouse Covered with Stucco
7. Restoration of Traditional Townhouse and Downtown Regeneration
8. Western-Style Townhouse
9. Townhouse with Western-Style Facade
10. Office Building in Port Town 1
11. Office Building in Port Town 2
12. Western-Style Factory and Church
13. Western-Style Station Building
14. History of Dwelling Culture in Tokyo
15. Retro Architecture
