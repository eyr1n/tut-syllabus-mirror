Objectives of class
Japanese
コンピュータのハードウェアの基本的な仕組みの一つとして，論理回路を学ぶ．論理回路の数学的基礎（ブール代数）を学んだ後，組み合わせ回路，順序回路，およびそれらの設計と応用について学ぶ．
English
The aim of this course is to understand the fundamentals of logic circuits. Topics covered are: Boolearn algebra, logic functions and logic gates, combinatorial logic circuits, and sequential logic circuits.

Japanese

10週目：フリップフロップ

13週目：状態数の最小化(2)

15週目：順序回路の応用

English
Week1: Boolean algebra.
Week2: Logic functions and their representations.
Week3: Shannon's expansion theorem and canonical forms.
Week4: Optimization of combinatorial circuits.
Week5: Optimization using Karnaugh maps.
Week6: Optimization using the Quine-McClusky method.
Week7: Combinatorial logic circuit examples.
Week8: Mid-term exam.
Week9: Representation of sequential circuits.
Week10: Flip-flops.
Week11: Design of sequential circuits.
Week12: State minimization (1).
Week13: State minimization (2).
Week14: Registers and counters.
Week15: Application of sequential logic circuits.
Week16: Final exam.

Goals to be achieved
Japanese
(1) 論理代数の定理が理解できる．
(2) 論理関数を標準形へ変換できる．
(3) 組み合わせ回路の最適化ができる．
(4) 基本的な組み合わせ回路を理解できる．
(5) フリップフロップなどの基本的な順序回路が理解できる．
(6) 順序回路の最適化ができる．
English
(1) To understand Boolean algebra.
(2) To learn how to convert logic functions to their canonical forms.
(3) To learn how to optimize combinatorial logic circuits.
(4) To understand basic combinatorial logic circuits.
(5) To understand basic sequential logic circuits.
(6) To leran how to optimize sequential logic circuits.

B: Achieved 80 % of goals and obtained total points of exam and reports, 70 or higher (out of 100 points).
C: Achieved 60 % of goals and obtained total points of exam and reports, 60 or higher (out of 100 points).
