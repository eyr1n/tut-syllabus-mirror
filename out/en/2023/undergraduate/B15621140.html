Objectives of class
Japanese
本授業は、3年次編入生の内、建築系以外の学科から編入してきた学生を対象にしている。
建築設計の初心者が、基本的な製図の描き方を理解し、トレースを通して図面の描き方を体得することを目標にする。著名建築を題材として、平面図や断面図に加えて透視図の基本を学んだ後、着彩ソフトを使用してプレゼンテーション資料として仕上げるとともに、建築計画的な空間の捉え方の素養を身につけ、基礎的な建築設計のセンスを養うことを図る。
English
Targeted students of this class are students who have never received an architectural education among admission into third grade.
The Objects of this class are made beginners of Architectural design understand basic drawing skills, and get Drawing skills through the trace.
You learn the basics of perspective view in addition to the plan view and sectional view, as a theme of famous architecture.
Then, you finish the drawings as presentation materials using the painting software, and you cultivate a sense of basic architectural design through getting the sophistication of the architectural planning.

English
The attitude toward the assignment, the submitted drawings and the presentation at the critique session will be evaluated comprehensively.
　S: All the achievement targets have been met and the total score (out of 100) of the test and report is 90 points or higher.
　A: 90% of the achievement objectives are achieved, and the total score of the test and report (out of 100) is 80 points or more.
　B: 80% of the achievement target is achieved, and the total score of the test and report (out of 100) is 70 points or more.
　C: 70% of the achievement target is achieved, and the total score of the test and report (out of 100) is 60 points or more.
