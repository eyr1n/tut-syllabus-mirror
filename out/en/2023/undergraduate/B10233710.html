Objectives of class
Japanese
・様々なタイプの英語の文法構文の成立条件について意味論的観点から考察を行う。
・分析手法として構文文法及びフレーム意味論のアプローチを想定し、「表現全体としての構文的意味」、
　「各構文内に生じる動詞の意味」、及び「他の共起要素の意味」との間における複雑な相互作用について議論を行う。
English
・The objective of this course is to consider the condition under which various English grammatical constructions are formed from the viewpoint of Semantics.
・More specifically, this course takes approaches on the basis of Construction Grammar and Frame Semantics, and discusses the complex interplay among "the constructional meaning of the whole expression", "the verb meaning", and "the meaning of other grammatical element".

Other information
Japanese

English
・This class is held in the first period. Some students might expect to come late for or be absent from this class so often simply because of not being early risers or living far away from the university.
Such students are strongly recommended to take other class.
・Students need to be constantly reminded that this class is not "a foreign language course" but "a humanities course". The students in this course are thus encouraged to consider some mechanisms underlying the English language rather than just learning English as a language.
