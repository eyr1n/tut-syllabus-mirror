Objectives of class
Japanese
1年次入学の学生に対して、３年次で編入学する高専生とスムーズに合流できるような基礎能力を身につけることを目的とする。高校までの一般教育と2年次以降の専門教育、研究活動との橋渡しをするとともに、工学的センスや問題解決の姿勢などを身に付ける。
English
The purpose of this course is for the students who enrolled from 1st year to acquire basic skills that will enable them to smoothly join with students of technical college, who will be enrolling directly into the third year. Besides serving as a bridge between general education of high school to specialized education and research from the second year onwards, students will also acquire engineering and problem-solving skills.
