Objectives of class
Japanese
Reading, Writing, Speaking, Listeningのバランスに配慮しつつ、これまでに身につけた英語をさらに強化する。また、英語を媒介として世界のさまざまな文化、ものの見方に触れる。
English
With concern for a balance of reading, writing, speaking and listening, we aim to build upon the existing English ability of each student. In addition, we hope to have students explore various cultures and ways of thinking through English.
