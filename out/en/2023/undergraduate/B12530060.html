Objectives of class
Japanese
電気機器の電磁誘導に基づく電気エネルギーと機械エネルギーとの変換について学ぶ。
English
The aim of this lecture is to learn about the conversion between electrical energy and mechanical energy in electrical equipment (motors, pawer generators, etc.) based on electromagnetic induction.

9th week: Synchronous power generator (Murakami)
10th week: Synchronous motor (Murakami)
11th week: Direct current motor (Inada)
12th week: Direct current power generator (Inada)
13th week: Linear motor (Takikawa)
14th week: Carbon neutral and electrical machinery (Takikawa)
15th week: Research and Development case studies (Takikawa)
