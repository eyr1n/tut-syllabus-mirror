Objectives of class
Japanese

Able to consider from basics about modern problems occurs in this highly technological society / advanced information society.
Able to do an argument and presentation more effectively.

Goals to be achieved
Japanese
１．科学技術が社会の中で様々な捉え方がなされていることを知ること
２．自分の専門領域の意義やあり方を、社会的空間的な広がりや時代的時間的広がり、あるいは役割分担の中で考えられるようになること
３．答えが1つに定まらない問題、問題自体が曖昧な問題について、議論できるようになること
English
1. Know various social and historical viewpoints of technology.
2. Able to consider about both the significance and the ideal method of the specialty domain of your own, in social and time expanse of the times, and from the viewpoint of the role.
3. Able to argue problems, that its answer will be not decided in one, or that is vague in itself.
