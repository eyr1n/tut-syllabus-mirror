English
In this class, a teacher with experience in 'Indoor Environment and Building Services' will speak about basic knowledge related to solar radiation, light and acoustic environment.
In the first half of classes, students learn properties and basics of insolation/light and acoustic environment (Instructor: M. Tajima). In the last half of classes, students carry out practical exercises and design calculations (Instructor: Y. Shimazaki).
For environmental design exercises, active-learning method will be utilized.

The contents are as follows:
Instructor: M. Tajima
1st week (In-person), Introduction about Building Environmental Engineering 2
2nd week (In-person), Insolation/ sunlight (1): Basics of insolation/ sunlight and solar position
3rd week (In-person), Insolation/ sunlight (2): Sunlight and shadow, Insolation and short-wave/ long-wave radiation
4th week (In-person), Natural light source, artificial light source (1): Principles and measurement of lighting, vision
5th week (In-person), Natural light source, artificial light source (2): Clear vision, daylighting, artificial lighting
6th week (In-person), Architectural acoustics (1): Aspect of sound
7th week (In-person), Exercises
8th week (In-person), Mid-term examination

Instructor: Y. Shimazaki
9th week (In-person), Insolation/ sunlight (3): Solar radiation and energy evaluation (1)
10th week (In-person), Insolation/ sunlight (4): Solar radiation and energy evaluation (2)
11th week (In-person), Natural light source, artificial light source (3): Calculation of illuminance
12th week (In-person), Natural light source, artificial light source (4): Lighting design calculation
13th week (In-person), Architectural acoustics (2): Indoor acoustics, sound insulation
14th week (on-demand), Environmental design exercises
15th week (In-person), Environmental design exercises
16th week (In-person), End-term examination-Light environment

Goals to be achieved
Japanese
１）日射・日影の基礎的事項について理解できる
２）室内光環境，照明，色彩計画の考え方と基本的計算ができる
３）室内音響計画・設計に関する基礎が理解でき，音響に関する基本的計算ができる
４）騒音評価法を理解し，騒音計を用いた環境騒音を測定できる
５）騒音防止計画が理解できる
６）コンピュータを用いた設計ツールを活用できる
English
Students should be able to do the following:
1) Understand basis of daylighting and sunshade.
2) Understand the concept of indoor lighting environment, lighting, color planning, and calculate the basic calculation.
3) Understand the basis of indoor acoustic planning and design, and calculate the basic calculations of indoor acoustic.
4) Understand the noise evaluation methods and measurement of environmental noise by using a sound level meter.
5) Understand noise level planning.
6) Utilize computer aided tools for designing
