Goals to be achieved
Japanese
１）分布定数線路を説明できる．
２）反射係数や等価係数を算出できる．
３）２端子対回路のインピーダンス・アドミタンス行列を算出できる．
４）S-parametersとスミスチャートの関係を理解し，回路素子の軌跡をスミスチャート上に描くことができる．
５）基本的な高周波回路素子を設計できる．
６）アンテナの動作原理を理解し，平面波を使って説明できる．
７）ワイヤレス電力伝送向けの回路を説明できる．
English
Understand the theory to:
1) Explain a distributed constant circuit.
2) Calculate the Reflection and transmission coefficients.
3) Calculate the impedance and admittance matrix of the two-port network.
4) Explain the relationship between S-parameters and Smith charts, and draw the trajectory of circuit elements on a Smith chart.
5) Design basic RF circuit elements.
6) Explain the principle of antenna operation using plane waves.
7) Explain the behavior of the circuit for wireless power transmission.
