Contents of class
Japanese
This class goes through a range of thematic aspects of practical English communication. The majority of classwork is done in groups, so students will learn various aspects of this: teamwork, leadership, defining and achieving tasks. Active participation is a major aspect of this class.

Weeks:
1-2. Greetings & classroom English
3-4. Responses
5 Personality
6-7. Describing impressions
8. Clothes & appearance
9-10. Illnesses
11. Places
12-13. Housing
14-15. Describing places
16. Final Exam
