Objectives of class
Japanese
本講義では、「日経サイエンス」の記事と原著SCIENTIFIC AMERICANの英語記事を読み比べ、科学英語に親しみ、読みこなす能力を身につける。
English
In this lecture, students will read and compare articles in Nikkei Science with English articles in the original SCIENTIFIC AMERICAN to become familiar with and acquire the ability to read and understand scientific English.
