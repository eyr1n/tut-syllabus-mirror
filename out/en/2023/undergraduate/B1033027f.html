Self Preparation and Review
Japanese
1) 各回のユニットを予習の上（20分ほど）講義に参加すること。
2) 新出熟語や単語を理解しておくこと。

English
1. Read/Listen the assigned chapter about 20 minutes before class.
2. Prepare for the Quiz which will be held at the beginning of the class.
3. Participate in the class discussion and activities.
4. Overview of the class for about 25 minutes after every classes.
